# Cheniere Energy (cheniere-energy)

Cheniere Energy is a leading producer and exporter of liquefied natural gas (LNG) in the United States. The company operates the Sabine Pass LNG terminal in Louisiana and the Corpus Christi LNG terminal in Texas, providing full-service LNG solutions including liquefaction, vessel loading, and regasification to customers worldwide.
